Café-ine
Café-ine is a delightful and cozy website dedicated to a charming coffee shop where coffee lovers can explore a wide range of beverages, from classic espresso shots to unique seasonal blends. The website offers a virtual experience that captures the warm, inviting atmosphere of the café, making visitors feel like they're stepping into a local favorite. Whether you're a regular customer or a first-time visitor, Café-ine provides everything you need to know about the coffee shop, including its menu, special offers, and the story behind its inception. It’s not just about coffee; it’s about community, comfort, and a shared love for the art of brewing the perfect cup.
Route Trace
Route Trace combines transport options and gives you the best route with live arrival times. Get step-by-step directions with live guidance as you travel! This application focuses on removing doubts about the arrival time and availability of transport vehicles, more specifically, a UV express and a bus.
Lo-La
Lo-La is a mobile application designed for the elderly and the hearing or visually impaired, providing assistance to navigate daily tasks, enhancing accessibility and improving quality of life. By leveraging advancements in information technology and addressing key concerns such as medication reminders, battery consumption, and language accessibility, the proposed digital companion endeavors to provide meaningful support that aligns with the evolving needs and preferences of aging individuals and individuals with impairments in the digital age.
